# Services Agreement

This Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2026, by and between Northwind Analytics, LLC ("Provider") and Harborview Retail, Inc. ("Client").

## 1. Scope of Services
Provider will deliver quarterly analytics reports and maintain a secure data dashboard for Client.

## 2. Term and Termination
The term begins on April 1, 2026 and continues for twelve (12) months. Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.

## 3. Fees and Payment
Client will pay $24,000 annually, invoiced quarterly in advance. Late payments accrue 1.5% interest per month.

## 4. Confidentiality
Each party will protect the other's confidential information and use it solely for performing obligations under this Agreement.

## 5. Data Security
Provider will maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Client data.

## 6. Force Majeure
Neither party will be liable for delays or failures caused by events beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, labor disputes, or government actions. The affected party will notify the other and resume performance as soon as practicable.

## 7. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

## 8. Entire Agreement
This Agreement represents the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes prior discussions.
